The Ottimo 4SV Bike

4SV stands for Four Season V Brake. The design brief for the 4SV was to build a no compromise road bike that could be used anytime, anywhere.

The 4SV has been thoroughly tested from the Kielder forest trails to pass storming in the Lake District with everything in between. The unique attributes of the 4SV mean that the only limits are your imagination.

Frame

Built from aerospace grade oversize Titanium, Ottimo frames are light, strong and come with a lifetime guarantee. Ti frames are not mass produced in a mould, but are made individually by craftsmen and can be specified to give a better fit for the average rider.

We offer four sizes with components chosen to suit.

Although the 4SV was not conceived as a race bike, the all up weight for the medium sized light wheel version is only 8.4 kilograms. This has been achieved through the use of ultralight components and translates into a fast and responsive ride.

Wheels

It was obvious right away that one pair of wheels just wasn't going to do everything that may be asked of the 4SV. To expect the same set of wheels to go on a tour of the Alps as well as a fast sportive was simply unrealistic.

Uniquely, the 4SV comes with the choice of two sets of wheels.

H Plus Son Archetype rims and Hope Mono hubs are handbuilt to the very highest standards with different spoke counts and tyres to provide a choice of wheels that can meet any challenge.

Fork and Brakes

Because we offer two wheelsets the frame, forks and brakes had to accommodate different tyre widths easily. We chose a short CX fork and TRP CX8.4 brakes. This combination not only provides astonishing stopping power, it also allows for tyres up to 32mm with fenders to be fitted.

Gearing

Getting a truly usable range of gears without using a triple was also a challenge. The answer was to fit a 46/30 double chainset. PMP are one of only a select few manufacturers to make such a combination. Not only does it function perfectly, it also has those wonderful Italian looks.

Campagnolo's Centaur/Athena transmission will provide many years of faultless service and used in conjunction with that PMP double provides a range of gears that will keep you both at the head of affairs and easily over the top of the steepest climb.

This combination of fork and brake also allows the use of 32mm tyres for proper off road adventures.
